Process - How we do it.

You give us the brief and we'll find the inspiration! We'll create and hone in on the design, choose the fabric and colour, silhouette and sample, assemble then test sample garmets with the client. After client approval we move to production, quality inspect, pack then deliver and, if required, maintain an inventory. PAINS

Ready-to-Wear

Not only do we design and supply custom uniforms but we also supply ready-to-wear uniforms that can be embroided with your logo. We supply the following sectors: corporate, hospitality, medical, childcare, trades, retail. We can put together a complete corporate wardrobe including accessories such as belts, ties and scarfs. Additionally, as mentioned above, we can mix custom design with ready-to-wear. For example, mix a custom design tunic with a ready-to-wear pant. Our suppliers include: Biz Collection, John Kevin, Field's Knitwear and others.

About Us

Supplying Businesses since 1994

Starting life as a successful party plan fashion business, we morphed into bespoke corporate uniforms after securing a contract to design, manufacture and supply a corporate uniform range to a multinational company. With 25+ years experience behind us, we continue to proudly offer a one-stop shop to support our wide range of clients and all of their corporate uniform, workwear and personal protective equipment (PPE) requirements. Our clients range from airlines to childcare centres, from sporting clubs to retail, private sector and local government organisations. We work closely with our clients to ensure your uniforms achieve the perfect combination of style and function. Our Values
