Custom-Design Uniforms

No two clients are the same, so we custom-design uniforms to project your distinctive corporate brand, integrity and image. Complete Solution Management

You give us the brief, and we'll find the inspiration! We work closely with our clients to ensure uniforms achieve the perfect combination of style and function. We'll create and hone in on the design, choose the fabric and colour, silhouette and sample, assemble then test sample garments with the client. After client approval, we move to production, quality inspection, pack, then deliver and, if required, maintain an inventory that you can access online. We take the pain out of uniform procurement!

About Us

Supplying Businesses since 1994

Starting as a successful party plan fashion business, we morphed into a custom design corporate uniform business after securing a large contract to design, manufacture and supply a corporate uniform range to a multinational company. With 25+ years of experience behind us, we proudly offer a one-stop shop to support our wide range of clients and all of their corporate uniform and workwear requirements. Our clients range from airlines to childcare centres, sporting clubs to retail, the private sector, and local government organisations. We work closely with our clients to ensure your uniforms achieve the perfect combination of style and function.

Our Mission

Since the beginning, we have strived to keep all parts of our business operation local, Local suppliers of fabric, notions, local manufacturers, and local TAFE college. We will keep all aspects of the fashion uniform manufacture and supply chain local in Australia and continue to do so.
